## Description
Plant something. Watch the world respond.

The Witness is a contemplative side-scrolling platformer where every seed you plant alters the landscape. Procedural terrain. Fractal trees. Mycelium networks that connect everything underground.

PROCEDURAL PERLIN TERRAIN
No two worlds are identical. The terrain is generated from Perlin noise — rolling hills, cliff faces, valleys, and plateaus that feel organic and explorable.

SEED PLANTING
Find seeds. Plant them. Watch fractal trees grow in real-time. Each tree connects to a mycelium network underground, feeding nutrients to nearby plants and reshaping the terrain.

MYCELIUM NETWORKS
Beneath the surface, fungal networks connect your plantings. Feed one tree and watch distant plants benefit. The underground world is as important as the surface.

DAY/NIGHT CYCLE
Light shifts. Colors change. Different plants thrive at different times. The world has rhythm, and learning that rhythm is part of the experience.

60FPS TOUCH CONTROLS
Responsive touch controls with smooth platforming physics. The game runs at 60fps with everything rendered in real-time Canvas — no sprites, no pre-made art.

A platformer that rewards patience over speed.
